Introduction to Narrative Text

A Narrative Text is a type of writing that aims to tell a story, whether it's fiction (like fairy tales and fables) or non-fiction (like biographies), with the main purpose of entertaining the reader.

1. The Purpose: The purpose of a Narrative Text is simple: to entertain the reader. Imagine yourself as a movie director.

2. The Structure: Every story has an orientation, complication, and resolution.

3. Language Focus: Use past tense verbs and time connectors like then, after that, finally.
